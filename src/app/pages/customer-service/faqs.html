FAQs

WHAT EXACTLY DOES 100% HANDCRAFTED MEAN?

100% handcrafted means that all of our products are made with our hands. From our caramel to the specially designed boxes that our tasty treats come in. We design each and every box with you in mind.

WHY DOES THE SHIPPING COST SO MUCH?

In order to deliver Trimble’s Treats to your door, we need to keep the temperature regulated. This requires extra steps in the packaging process which adds cost to the total shipping. As always, with an order of $80 or more, shipping is free!

HAS TRIMBLE’S TREATS CONISDERED MAKING SUGAR FREE OPTIONS?

We are in the process of creating sugar free, fat free and gluten free recipes without compromising the great taste you have come to know and love with Trimble’s Treats.

WHY DO YOU PARTNER WITH BIG BROTHERS BIG SISTERS?

Olivia fell in love with the organization when she interend there in college. “Big Brothers Big Sisters impacts communities independently while working together to make the world a safer, more loving place for everything, especially children. I can’t think of a better way to give back to not only my community but your community as well.” As a result, 5% of every sale Trimble’s Treats makes is donated to Big Brothers Big Sisters.

WHY IS THERE 3 DAY HANDLING?

We beleive in handcrafted which means that every order is made to order just for you. From the time you order, you will have your treats in less than a week time and custom made just for you.

WHATS UP WITH THE GIFT BOXES?

Olivia is passionate about design so she creates a new box each season for Trimble’s Treats for the times that you “treat yo’self” or friends. For those other times when there is a special occastion involved, such as a birthday, you can choose a specially designed birthday box to show up on your loved ones doorstep. Check back often for new boxes!

ARE YOUR TREATS MADE IN NUT-FREE FACILITY?

Trimble’s Treats are made in a home-kitchen therefore our items that contain nuts are created in the same kitchen that create our other products. This also means that our kitchen has not been inspected by the FDA.
